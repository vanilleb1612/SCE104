Battleship

Rules:

"Battleship is a war-themed board game for two players in which the opponents try to guess the location of the other's various ships." (Source) Players take turns firing shots to attack enemy ships. The first player to destroy all the enemy ships wins. Yellow indicates an unharmed cell with a boat in it. Orange indicates a cell that has been hit with a boat in it. Red indicates a cell with a sunken boat in it. Blue indicates a cell where a shot has been fired but there was no boat.
